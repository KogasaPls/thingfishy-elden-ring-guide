The Smart Way To Start Every Build: Early Smithing & Somber Stone Route

A written walkthrough of Thingfishy’s “The Smart Way To Start EVERY Build” (YouTube, lxfZdm6PO0I). It’s a pre-boss “setup” route: before you fight anything or commit to a build, you ride a lap of the map picking up nearly every free Golden Seed, Sacred Tear, and early Smithing/Somber Smithing Stone, then cash in two soft, low-risk kills for a huge rune payout. Do this once on a fresh character and every subsequent build starts most of the way to fully upgraded.

Timestamped links point back to the source clip. If a step reads unclear, the video is the tiebreaker.

How to use this guide

  • Bold marks an item, Grace, or named location the moment you should pick it up or reach it.
  • Each numbered leg covers one contiguous stretch of riding; do them in order.
  • Screenshots show the actual in-game pickup toast or Grace banner where one was visible in the source footage. Treat those as confirmed. Anything else (dragon names, minor location labels) is best-effort from the narration and general game knowledge.
  • Optional detours, dangers, and pure-Somber-build skips are called out in colored callout boxes: gold for a tip, rust for a warning, green for a skip.

Chapter 1: Golden Seeds, Sacred Tears & Somber Smithing Stones

0:00 · watch

1. Limgrave: Church of Elleh & Gatefront

watch

  1. At the Church of Elleh, grab Smithing Stone [1] off the ground and speak to the merchant to acquire Torrent.
  2. Ride to the camp at Gatefront Ruins: loot the Flask of Wondrous Physic from the back of the carriage south of camp, then quit out (resets the camp’s aggro) and duck into the cellar for the Whetstone Knife.

2. South to Weeping Peninsula

watch

Ride the main road all the way south to Weeping Peninsula, picking up on the way:

  • Smithing Stone [1] on a corpse on the first bridge, mind your footing.
  • A Somber Smithing Stone [1] held by an NPC sitting in a chair just past the bridge.
  • A Gold-Pickled Fowl Foot up the hill on the far side of the road.

Continuing south, the next bridge has more Smithing Stones and a Stonesword Key. In the valley just after, loot the Morning Star from the carriage on the left, plus another Smithing Stone.

3. Castle Morne Rampart & the coast

watch

  1. Light the Grace at Castle Morne Rampart.
  2. Head south for a Golden Seed: hug the wall on your right on the way in and the nearby Giant won’t spot you, no arrow-dodging required.
  3. Grab the regional map while you’re here.
  4. Continue south to the beach for a Somber Smithing Stone.

Quit out, return to Castle Morne Rampart Grace, then head up the hill and around to the right for Callu Baptismal Church, your first Sacred Tear.

4. The three remaining Weeping Peninsula churches

watch

  1. Fast travel to Castle Morne Rampart, then ride northwest hugging the cliffs on your left, across the bridge, to the Church of Pilgrimage for a second Sacred Tear, plus the Bleed Grease from the graveyard just outside.

  2. Ride southwest to the Fourth Church of Marika for a third Sacred Tear.

    5. Back to Limgrave: Warmaster’s Shack & the Golden Vow

watch

  1. Fast travel to the Limgrave gatefront and ride up the hill, grabbing a Golden Seed and lighting Warmaster’s Shack Grace. (This route also stashes a spare Stonesword Key here to save a later trip.)

  2. From Warmaster’s Shack, head northeast between the cliffs and ruins for the Strength-knot Crystal Tear.

  3. Jump down the rocks and keep going northeast.

    The rest of this route can be done without fighting anything, but it’s worth pausing here: take down the mounted knight overlooking the mob camp (northeast of Warmaster’s Shack, between the ruined structure and the cliff edge) for the Golden Vow Ash of War, a strong buff for almost any build.

  4. Drop into the nearby camp for a Golden Rune and the Exalted Flesh.

6. Saints Bridge to the Third Church of Marika

watch

  1. Take the road out of camp to Saints Bridge, grabbing Smithing Stones as you cross.

  2. Ride past the wandering merchant, southeast to the ruins bordering the Mistwood. Loot the graves for the Nomadic Warrior’s Cookbook [1] (you’ll use it to craft Sleep Pots for the rune farm in Chapter 3) and pick up two Golden Runes.

  3. Drop down the cliff to the Third Church of Marika (northeastern Limgrave, right on the Caelid border) for the Wondrous Physic and another Sacred Tear. A hidden Sending Gate in the bushes behind the church warps straight to the Bestial Sanctum in Dragonbarrow. You’ll use it two steps from now.

7. Fort Haight & the Dectus Medallion (left half)

watch

  1. Ride south through the Mistwood to Fort Haight (southeast corner of Limgrave). Grab the Golden Seed out front.

  2. Climb the ladder to the top for the Dectus Medallion (Left).

  3. Quit out for safety, walk back to the Third Church of Marika, and use the hidden Sending Gate to warp to the Bestial Sanctum in Dragonbarrow.

    8. Dragonbarrow: Lenne’s Rise & Fort Faroth

watch

  1. Ride south from the Bestial Sanctum, grabbing a Golden Seed on the way, then follow the shown route down through the branches and across the bridge to light Lenne’s Rise Grace.

  2. Climb the spirit spring behind it and loot the graveyard for three Golden Runes.

  3. Head southwest past the minor Erdtree, up another spirit spring, and light the Grace outside Fort Faroth on the eastern Dragonbarrow cliffs. The dragon Tarn Farosh is known to patrol this stretch, so stay alert.

  4. Climb the fort’s ladder for the Dectus Medallion (Right), jump down the second hole for Golden Rune [12] by the rats, then jump across and down for Radagon’s Soreseal.

  5. Quit out, return to the Grace outside, jump the smoldering wall, and take the nearby spirit spring down to the Church of Plague for another Sacred Tear.

9. The Divine Tower of Caelid

watch

Walk back to Fort Faroth, skirt around the large dragon, and head west toward the Divine Tower of Caelid.

  1. Down the road to the right: Somber Smithing Stone [9].

  2. Double back and climb to the tower’s upper platform. A Teardrop Scarab sits here: rather than fighting it, kick it off the cliff for a Somber Smithing Stone [8] and stay clear of its death explosion.

10. Back to Stormhill and down into Liurnia

watch

  1. Return to Stormhill (Limgrave) and head north to the broken bridge. Drop down and follow the cliffs into Liurnia of the Lakes, lighting the Grace by the lake facing the cliffs.

  2. Grab a Sacred Tear from the nearby lakeside church.

  3. Head out the west side, ride down past the camp, and at the wandering merchant pop a Golden Rune [6] and buy out all his available Smithing Stones.

11. Liurnia ruins to Raya Lucaria Academy

watch

  1. From the merchant, follow the main path to the ruins by the Academy causeway, and grab the Smithing Stones next to the teleporter gazebo before using it.

  2. Light the South Raya Lucaria Gate Grace.

    Double-jump off the bridge onto the rock below for a couple more Smithing Stones. Skip this if you’re going pure Somber; it’s a slight detour for stones you won’t need.

  3. Hug the cliffs to your right until the dragon comes into view, then grab the Academy Glintstone Key without aggroing it. Open your map and walk back to the Grace, then head into the Academy.

  4. Light the Academy courtyard Grace, then cross toward the bridge for another Golden Seed before entering Raya Lucaria proper.

12. Through Raya Lucaria to the Abductor Virgin shortcut

watch

  1. Time your rolls to dodge the Glintstone Bouncers’ spells, then quit out at the door to reset them.
  2. Run the dungeon (grab the extra Grace along the way if you want it). After the bridge, drop down for the Carian Knight Armor, solid protection for the rest of this run.
  3. Navigate the rocks toward the lift, staying tight to the left to dodge the pursuing dogs (they rarely actually catch you).
  4. At the top, dash in to light Schoolhouse Classroom Grace, then head back out, keeping left to drop by the water mill onto the descending platform.
  5. When you see the tree on the right of the screen, drop down and let the Abductor Virgin statue grab you, a free one-way teleport into Volcano Manor.

13. Volcano Manor: Somber Smithing Stones

watch

  1. On arrival, equip the Carian Knight set for safety, then follow the shown jump off the platform exactly to minimize fall damage (spam roll on landing if you catch a bad one; it’s survivable).

    New to this route? Level your flasks as you go in case you need heals here.

  2. Jump onto the building ahead and loop around it for two more Smithing Stones. It’s faster and safer than dropping straight into the lava.

  3. Cross the lava, loop around the next building, for Somber Smithing Stone [6], then down and up the stairs.

  4. If the fire-breathing serpent is near the lift, quit out to reset its position. Off the lift, head right for Somber Smithing Stone [5].

  5. Up the stairs to open a shortcut for later, then let the nearby monk kill you, the fastest way back to the start of this section.

14. The Academy Crystal Cave

watch

  1. On respawn, run left across the lava pools to a linear ~90-second cave path leading to a Grace. Resting here means you’re no longer trapped in the area.

  2. Fast travel back to Liurnia and enter the Academy Crystal Cave via the lake ruins: open the door, then the chest, hopping on top of the chest right after opening it for extra safety.

    This cave is the most dangerous part of the whole route. Take it slow.

  3. Out of the spawn room: two Smithing Stones on the rocks, one more behind the nearby shack.

    Speedrunner tip: jump-block, jump-block on the uphill sections; it’s the fastest way to move here.

  4. A quick quit-out here staggers the Servants of Rot guarding the path, giving you a real shot at the next Smithing Stone [5]. This section is RNG-dependent: anywhere from 2 to 20 minutes depending on luck.

  5. Jump over the rooftop into the rest of the cave for another Smithing Stone [5], across the beam for two more, then quit out again.

  6. On reload, head to the ladder and up it for Somber Smithing Stone [4], then into the final room for one more Smithing Stone [5].

  7. Rest at the Grace and head back to the Academy Gate Grace.

    Skipping this cave: buy Somber Smithing Stone [4] from the Twin Maiden Husks at the Roundtable Hold instead. The video usually eats that cost rather than risk the cave.

15. Wrapping up Liurnia

watch

  1. Through the teleporting sign, light the East Raya Lucaria Gate Grace.

  2. Follow the shown route down the spirit spring for a Somber Smithing Stone [3] from a seated NPC, then walk back and on to Bellum Church for the last Sacred Tear of this chapter.

    Somber weapon users are done here: skip straight to Chapter 3.

Chapter 2: Additional Smithing Stones

17:16 · watch

16. Altus Plateau

  1. Ride the Divine Tower lift up to Altus Plateau and head for the capital. Up the stairs: two Golden Seeds at the bottom, then light the Grace and continue up for two more.

  2. Fast travel back to the bottom, then ride east toward the Sealed Tunnel.

    17. The Sealed Tunnel

  3. Light the Grace, go through the hidden wall, and follow the shown route through successive rooms for a Smithing Stone [5] and the Smithing-Stone Miner’s Bell Bearing [1].

  4. Continue: off the back of the lift for a cheeky Smithing Stone [5], across the roots for another in the next room, then for the final one jump onto the ledge and drop to the bottom for Golden Rune [9]. Let the nearby enemies handle your exit.

  5. Respawn at the Grace (not the Stake of Marika) and head back out to the South Raya Lucaria Gate.

18. Liurnia mop-up

  1. Ride toward the courtyard, grabbing a Smithing Stone [3] on the way, drop down by the crab for another, and round the corner for the final Golden Seed.

  2. Head southeast to Gate Town Grace, then ride southwest across the lake to the Boilprawn Shack.

  3. A little further on, grab the Dexterity-knot Crystal Tear.

  4. Head northwest for more Smithing Stones, quitting out when you drop past the lobsters, then fast travel back to Gate Town Grace for the final stones: ride toward the distinctive rock formation in the distance for stones by the flowers, then northeast to the poison gazebo for the last ones.

  5. Travel to the Roundtable Hold and spend every rune you have on the four remaining Smithing Stone [3]s and all twelve Smithing Stone [4]s from the Twin Maiden Husks.

Chapter 3: The Rune Farm

20:17 · watch

Two low-risk kills, no real fights, for the bulk of your ~140-150k runes.

19. Night’s Cavalry at Lenne’s Rise

watch

  1. Travel to Lenne’s Rise and wait for nightfall: this is when the roaming Night’s Cavalry spawns.
  2. Jump onto the side of the bridge and quit out positioned in front of the gap in the wall.
  3. Reload. The Cavalry will often charge straight off the bridge toward you and fall to its death for a clean kill and a large rune reward, no real fight required. If it does something else instead, quit out and try the reload again; it can take a couple of attempts to land the favorable spawn.

20. Elder Dragon Greyoll at Fort Faroth

watch

The second target is the Elder Dragon Greyoll, a mostly-passive giant dragon on the Dragonbarrow cliffs near Fort Faroth worth a massive rune payout: tens of thousands of runes for what amounts to a Bleed-tick war of attrition, not a real fight.

  1. Head to Fort Faroth. Equip the Dexterity-knot Crystal Tear in your Physick, along with the Bleed Grease and a Gold-Pickled Fowl Foot.
  2. Approach Greyoll from her left flank, around the mid-point of her tail. That keeps you out of the roar that alerts her brood of lesser dragons, so you can work uninterrupted.
  3. Drink the Physick, apply the Bleed Grease to your weapon (the video uses the Morning Star flail here for its Spinning Chain skill), then hold Spinning Chain until your stamina runs out, let it recover, and repeat. The continuous hits stack Bleed procs that chip a percentage of Greyoll’s health with every proc.
  4. This loop is intentionally low-stakes: even on the squishier starting classes (Prophet, Wretch, Confessor, Hero) you’ve got a generous window to whittle her down, and the Morning Star is there in reserve if anything goes wrong.
  5. Just as Greyoll dies, pop the Gold-Pickled Fowl Foot to boost the rune payout from the kill.

That’s the full setup: a +7 flask with 9 charges, full Smithing Stone stock to +16, Somber stock to +6, and enough runes for roughly level 40. All without a single boss fight.
